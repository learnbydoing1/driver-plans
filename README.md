# Driver Plans

**Driver subscription product — keep 100% of your earnings with a flat weekly or monthly plan.**

Driver Plans is a subscription landing page for ride-hailing drivers. Instead of paying per-ride commission, drivers select a flat-rate weekly or monthly plan and keep every dollar they earn. The page is fully bilingual (Arabic and English) with native RTL support.

---

## What it does

- **Plan comparison** — side-by-side weekly and monthly subscription cards with pricing, features, and selection.
- **Payment flow** — integrated payment form for plan activation.
- **Bilingual UI** — full Arabic and English with automatic RTL layout switching.
- **Benefits section** — communicates the value proposition: zero commission, higher earnings, instant activation.
- **Responsive** — mobile-first design for drivers on the go.

## The product concept

Traditional ride-hailing platforms take 15-25% commission per ride. This product flips the model: drivers pay a predictable flat fee and retain 100% of their fare earnings. The landing page is designed to convert drivers from commission-based to subscription-based plans.

## Tech stack

- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Internationalization:** Custom i18n context with RTL/LTR switching
- **Animations:** Custom animation utilities
